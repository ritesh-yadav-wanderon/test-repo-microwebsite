Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about safe travel with WanderOn in 2026.

Why should I avoid Europe this summer?

Due to the escalating Iran-Israel-US conflict, European airspace is experiencing disruptions, and the proximity to conflict zones poses potential safety concerns. We recommend safer alternatives in Southeast Asia and island nations.

Are Southeast Asia destinations completely safe?

Yes! Destinations like Bali, Thailand, Vietnam, Singapore, and the Maldives are far from conflict zones and maintain excellent safety records. These countries have stable governments and robust tourism infrastructure.

What if I already booked Europe travel?

Contact our team immediately. We offer flexible rescheduling to safer destinations without additional charges, subject to airline and hotel policies. Our travel experts will help you find the perfect alternative.

How is WanderOn ensuring traveler safety?

We monitor global situations 24/7, maintain direct communication with local partners, provide travel insurance, offer 24/7 emergency support, and only operate in destinations with verified safety standards.

Will flight costs be higher for these alternatives?

Actually, flights to Southeast Asia from India are often cheaper and shorter than Europe. You'll save 30–40% on average while enjoying closer destinations with better weather.

What's included in WanderOn packages?

Our packages include accommodations, guided tours, airport transfers, select meals, activities, and 24/7 support. Flight bookings can be arranged separately or as part of the package.

Can I customize my itinerary?

Absolutely! We offer both fixed group departures and customized private tours. Contact our travel experts to create a personalized itinerary based on your preferences and budget.
